E9TH Tokenomics

Complete overview of token distribution, allocation, and deflationary mechanics.

Token Details

  • Ticker: E9TH
  • Chain: Solana (SPL / Token-2022 compatible)
  • Total Supply: 10,000,000,000
  • Initial Price: $0.00005
  • Decimals: 9
  • Model: Deflationary

Allocation

Distribution Breakdown

  • Public Sale — 40% (4,000,000,000 E9TH)
    Available for public purchase at launch. No vesting period.
  • Team & Founders — 20% (2,000,000,000 E9TH)
    24-month linear vesting starting 6 months after launch.
  • Marketing & Rewards — 15% (1,500,000,000 E9TH)
    Community rewards, partnerships, and marketing initiatives.
  • Liquidity — 15% (1,500,000,000 E9TH)
    Initial liquidity provision and market making.
  • Treasury — 10% (1,000,000,000 E9TH)
    Staking rewards pool and future development funding.

Deflationary Mechanics

  • Burn Rate: 0.5% on all transfers (50 basis points)
  • Burn Destination: Tokens are permanently removed from circulation
  • Exemptions: Staking rewards and airdrops are not subject to burn
  • Impact: Gradually reduces total supply over time, increasing scarcity

Staking Rewards

  • Source: Treasury allocation (1,000,000,000 E9TH)
  • APY: Variable based on staking participation
  • Minimum Stake: 1,000 E9TH
  • Unstaking Period: 7 days
  • Rewards: Distributed daily from the treasury pool

Governance & Security

  • Multisig Ownership: 3-of-5 multisig for critical operations
  • Emergency Pause: Ability to halt transfers if needed
  • Blacklist Protection: On-chain blacklist for malicious addresses
  • Transparency: All operations logged on-chain

Roadmap & Milestones

  • Phase 1: Token launch, staking v1, website & litepaper
  • Phase 2: Partner integrations, holder dashboard, burn tracker
  • Phase 3: Expanded marketplace, mobile app, scaling rewards
